Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing pressurized hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It has a thick consistency and strong flavor, typically used as a base for many other coffee beverages like lattes, cappuccinos, and more.

Ingredients:
  • Freshly ground espresso beans
  • Hot water
Nutritional Information:
  • Calories: 5
  • Caffeine: 63 mg
  • Sugar: 0 g
